DOAC Exclusions

  • Surgery Expected
  • Cannot Tolerate Oral Medications
  • Current Treatment with itraconazole, ketoconazole, phenytoin, or rifampin
  • Child Pugh Class C
  • History of Hypersensitivity Reactions or Treatment Failure

Specific Rivaroxaban Exclusions

  • High Risk Antiphospholipid Syndrome
  • Child Pugh B or C Liver Disease
  • Valvular Heart Disease
